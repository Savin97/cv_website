How it works

  1. Model ingests historical stock data for a list of stocks from a custom, proprietary database.
  2. Engineers relevant statistical and financial features from this data.
  3. Scores each ticker and generates risk alerts.
  4. Presents the results in a clean, actionable dashboard.

Example output

For each ticker and earnings date, you’ll see:

  • Historical average move on earnings
  • Std dev / volatility of reactions
  • Recent EPS surprise vs. price reaction
  • Risk score and alert label

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. This is a research and analytics tool. It helps you understand historical risk patterns around earnings events. Nothing on this site or in the product is a recommendation to buy or sell any security.

Who is this for?

Primarily for portfolio managers, serious retail traders, and analysts who want a more systematic view of earnings-linked risk.

What will it cost?

During the beta phase, access will be free. Planned pricing later is in the $29–$49/month range for individual users.
